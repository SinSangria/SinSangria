About

Sin Sangria is a demon who has left hell for the mortal realm and taken up VTubing to inconspicuously interact with the mortal realm to learn about mortals and encourage chaos!

Sin enjoys Art and Tech. Sin can often be seen creating digital art, coding, or learning something new. And has plans to learn: Animation, Guitar, Ukelele, Singing, and Tech topics. See Sin stuggle at all of the above live on Twitch! Weekly schedule coming soon!

Lore

Notorious for the ability to tempt mortals into committing acts of wanton debauchery and creative indulgence, Sin Sangria grew tired of life in Hell and wished to join in on creative indulgence with the mortals.

Sin admired the creativity, expression, and willingness to challenge conventions that artists embodied. Sin Sangria felt that the mortals who dedicated their lives to artistic pursuits were kindred spirits - unafraid to defy societal norms and embrace their darkest impulses in the name of their craft.

Thus, driven by a thirst for artistic enlightenment, Sin made the daring decision to traverse into the mortal realm. With a hunger for connection and a craving for creative companionship, Sin sought out artists to befriend, eager to partake in the intoxicating euphoria of artistic friendship.

Surviving on a diet of blood, Sin devised an arrangement for sustainance amidst the mortals. Occasionally, Sin would forge demon contracts with select individuals, offering them infernal patronage in exchange for artworks of exquisite beauty. These creations, infused with the essence of Sin's otherworldly influence, would fetch currency in the mortal realm – currency that could then be exchanged for goods and services like blood packs.
